System Overview

The Smart Port Information System (SPIS) is a centralized platform designed to manage the complexities of modern port operations. By utilizing real-time IoT data and advanced predictive analytics, SPIS provides a comprehensive view of vessel traffic, berth allocation, and resource utilization.

From automated scheduling to predictive maintenance alerts, the system serves as the digital backbone for the harbor master and terminal operators.


Key Highlights:

It integrates all key data from internal port statistics to the public-facing vessel tracking tool, providing a single source of truth for all stakeholders.
